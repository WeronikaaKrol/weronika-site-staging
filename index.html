Vision

My goal after MIT is to make finance, data, and AI more practical and accessible in Poland

I want to return to Poland with strong international training and practical experience, then use both to support people and institutions that often have less access to high-quality knowledge, tools, and networks. My goal is to help make financial thinking, data literacy, and practical technology more useful in everyday decisions, career development, and smaller-scale innovation.

Financial education

Help students and young professionals better understand personal finance, investing, and financial decision-making, especially where practical financial education is still limited.

From ideas to action

Encourage young people to turn ideas into action by using data, technology, and financial knowledge to build projects, careers, and businesses of their own.

AI for smaller organizations

Help make practical AI tools more accessible to small businesses, local initiatives, and smaller institutions, so innovation is not limited to large companies with bigger resources.
